The Honourable

Kelly O'Dwyer

Former Member For Higgins

ABOUT

Kelly is currently the Minister for Jobs and Industrial Relations and Minister for Women. Since 2015 she has been a member of Cabinet and also Cabinet’s Expenditure Review Committee (ERC).

She has served as Minister for Revenue and Financial Services; Minister Assisting the Prime Minister for the Public Service; Assistant Treasurer; Minister for Small Business; and Parliamentary Secretary to the Treasurer.

In her portfolio roles Kelly has helped cut both company and personal income tax; made significant reforms to the financial services sector; announced a $328 million record funding package to combat domestic violence against women and girls; and made the biggest reform to employment services in 20 years.

She is also proud to have introduced the first ever Women’s Economic Security Statement – a $119 million package to help women and girls build their financial security.

She is an Ovarian Cancer Australia Ambassador, Patron of the Stonnington City Brass and Patron of the East Malvern Girls Football Club.

This decision has been made both harder, and easier, because of my admiration for, and friendship with the Prime Minister and my conviction that he will lead our Government to victory in May.
