APA Guide

Book/Report Referencing
Auther Year Of Publication Title + Subtitle Series Title and Volume Edition Place of Publication Publisher Pages
last name, first initial. (year). title subtitle. volume number. (edition.) suburb. state: publisher, page span
Lewis, J. (2017). How to not fail: IT Vol 1.,(4th ed.) Melbourne, VIC: James inc, 1-10.
Webpage/Article Referencing
Auther Year of Publication Title of Webpage Name of Web Host URL
last name, first name, (year). title, host name. Retrived from URL
Lewis, James, (2017). Do you hate APA? So do I!, James inc. Retrieved from http://jameslewis.com.au
